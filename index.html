Step-by-Step: How to Top Up a Mobile Phone with Crypto on Crypto Refills

  1. Go to the official Crypto Refills website — use a bookmarked URL. Navigate to the Mobile Top-Up section.
  2. Enter the recipient's phone number with the country code. The platform automatically identifies the carrier and shows available top-up denominations.
  3. Select the top-up amount — choose from available denominations (e.g. $5, $10, $20 equivalent). The amount is shown in both local currency and USD.
  4. Choose your cryptocurrency — select the coin you want to pay with. The equivalent crypto amount at the current rate is displayed immediately.
  5. Review the total cost — confirm the product amount, service fee, and crypto equivalent before proceeding. No account creation is required at this step.
  6. Send the exact crypto amount to the generated payment address within the payment window (typically 15–60 minutes depending on the coin). Send from your wallet — not from an exchange, as exchange withdrawal delays can cause timeouts.
  7. Wait for on-chain confirmation — once the required confirmations are reached, the top-up is applied to the phone instantly. You receive a confirmation email or on-screen notification.
Critical: Always send crypto from a personal wallet — not directly from an exchange. Exchange withdrawals can take 10–30 minutes and may cause your payment window to expire. If the window expires, contact Crypto Refills support with your transaction hash before sending again.

Best Practices for Using Crypto Refills Safely and Cost-Effectively

  • Always verify the phone number or email before paying — deliveries are instant and irreversible. A single digit error in the phone number means the credit goes to someone else permanently.
  • Pay with stablecoins for predictability — USDT or USDC eliminates the risk of price movement between order and payment. You pay exactly what the checkout shows.
  • Use low-fee chains for small purchases — for top-ups under $20, use Litecoin, XRP, MATIC, or TRC-20 USDT to keep network fees proportional to the purchase value.
  • Send from a personal wallet, not an exchange — exchange withdrawals introduce unpredictable delays that can cause payment window timeouts. Always send from MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or another self-custody wallet.
  • Copy-paste the payment address and amount — never type crypto addresses or amounts manually. One digit error sends funds to the wrong address or underpays, both potentially unrecoverable.
  • Verify gift card regional validity before purchasing — check whether the card works in the recipient's country and on their specific account region.
  • Bookmark the official Crypto Refills URL and verify the domain before each session — phishing sites targeting crypto payment services are common, particularly for high-value gift card purchases.

Troubleshooting Crypto Refills: Delayed Delivery, Wrong Number, and Payment Issues

"My top-up was paid but the phone didn't receive credit"

  • Wait a few minutes — some carriers apply credit with a short delay after confirmation. Check the phone balance after 5–10 minutes.
  • Confirm the on-chain transaction has reached the required confirmations. Payments with very low network fees may still be awaiting confirmation.
  • Contact Crypto Refills support with your transaction hash and the order reference. Do not initiate a second payment before resolving the first.

"I entered the wrong phone number"

  • If the top-up has already been applied, this is typically irreversible — the credit belongs to whoever owns that number. Contact support immediately with proof of the error; some cases may be partially resolved depending on the carrier.
  • Always verify the number before paying. Use the platform's number validation feature to confirm the carrier and country match your intent.

"My payment window expired before I could send"

  • Do not send the crypto after the window expires — the system may not process it. Contact Crypto Refills support with your details and wait for their guidance.
  • If you have already sent on-chain, provide the transaction hash to support immediately — they can often manually process transactions that arrive after the window if the transaction is verifiable on-chain.
Support contact: Use the official support channel linked in the Crypto Refills app or official website. Never share your wallet seed phrase or private key with any support contact — this information is never needed for order resolution.
